Welcome to KareGivv

KareGivv offers personalized home healthcare by connecting families with vetted caregivers. Whether you need care for a loved one or want to provide your services, join the KareGivv community today.

About KareGivv

KareGivv is a revolutionary home health care platform designed to meet the diverse needs of patients, including those with chronic illnesses. Our app brings non-emergency medical services directly to your doorstep, ensuring that you receive the care you need, when you need it, in the comfort of your own home. By connecting directly with qualified and vetted caregivers, patients can manage daily living activities, ongoing health challenges, and receive respite care without the additional costs associated with caregiver agencies. We value integrity, excellence, and a community driven approach in everything we do.

Why Choose KareGivv

Comprehensive Services: From wound care to daily living assistance, tailored to your needs.

Qualified Professionals: Certified and experienced caregivers dedicated to exceptional care.

Convenient Access: Easily request services through our user-friendly platform.

Cost-Effective Solutions: Quality care without high costs.

Our Services

We provide a wide range of home healthcare services to meet diverse needs:

Personal Care Assistance:
Help with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, and grooming.
Medical Care:
Medication management, and chronic disease management.
Respite Care:
Temporary relief for primary caregivers.
Ambulation Assistance:
Help with walking and mobility.
Feeding Assistance:
Support with eating and meal preparation.
Elderly Care:
Specialized care for seniors.
Palliative Care:
Comfort care for serious illnesses.
General Convalescence:
Recovery support after illness or surgery.
Postpartum Care:
Assistance for new mothers
